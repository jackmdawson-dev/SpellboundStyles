Nails- Included in the services below however you may book in separately if you feel they need done before.

Bath & Brush- This involves a bath and blowdry as well as a brush out with slicker brush and comb. Nails included.

Bath & Tidy up- This includes a bath, blowdry, cleanup of sanatary areas using clippers, neatening of around the paws, tail and eyes with scissors as well as nail cuts when needed.

Bath & Deshed- This includes a bath using a special deshed shampoo that helps lift the dead undercoat which is further removed through the dryer and deshed tools following. Nail cuts are included.

Bath & Puppy Cut- This is similar to a bath and tidy up but is for dogs under 6 months and is designed to be repeated several times before their first full groom, so as to get them trained to enjoy the grooming process.

Bath & Full Groom- This includes a bath, blowdry, length removed based on your specification of the topcoat with clippers, cleanup of sanatary area and pads with clippers, neatening everything else with scissors including legs, bum, tail, ears, face. Nails included.

Factors that affect the pricing are coat condition, required time for service and clean-up, skill required for type of groom, temperament as well as the breed and weight of your dog. Excess in matting, difficult temperment as well as being late on drop off or pick up may incur an extra charge.
