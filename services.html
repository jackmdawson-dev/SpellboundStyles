Services

I offer washes, full grooms, puppy grooms, and nail cutting

pricing is based off of coat condition, required time for service and clean-up, skill required, temperament as well as the breed and weight of your dog

Bath & Deshed- This includes and bath using a special deshed shampoo that helps lift the dead undercoat which is further removed through the dryer and deshed tools following. Nail cuts are included.

Bath & Tidy up- This includes a bath, blowdry, cleanup of sanatary areas using clippers, neatening of around the paws, tail and eyes with scissors as well as nail cuts when needed.

Bath & Puppy Cut- This is similar to a bath and tidy up but is for dogs under 6 months and is designed to be repeated several times before their first full groom, so as to get them trained to enjoy the grooming process.

Bath & Full Groom- This includes a bath, blowdry, length removed based on your specification of the topcoat with clippers, cleanup of sanatary area and pads with clippers, neatening everything else with scissors including legs, bum, tail, ears, face. Nails included.

Bath & Brush/Blowdry- This involves a bath and blowdry and where applicable, a brush out. Nails included.
